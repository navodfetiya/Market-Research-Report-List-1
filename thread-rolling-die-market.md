Flat thread rolling dies produce threads by compressing a flat strip of material, while planetary thread rolling dies utilize a rotating mechanism to create threads on various shapes and sizes. Cylindrical thread rolling dies are designed for cylindrical workpieces, offering efficiency in thread formation. Each type caters to specific application needs, enhancing manufacturing processes across industries.</p></p>
