<p><p>The Logic Output Photocouplers market encompasses three main types: AC, DC, and Logic Inverters. AC photocouplers relay signals in alternating current applications, ensuring isolation in diverse electrical environments. DC photocouplers operate with direct current, ideal for low-voltage digital circuits. Logic Inverters, on the other hand, reverse the logic state of input signals, enhancing circuit functionality. These components are crucial in providing electrical isolation, signal integrity, and protection in various electronic systems across industries like telecommunications and industrial automation.</p></p>
