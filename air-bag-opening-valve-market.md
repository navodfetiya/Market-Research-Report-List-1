<p><ul><li>Continuous Airbag Opening Valve</li><li>Metering Bag Opening Valve</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Air Bag Opening Valve market consists of two main types: Continuous Airbag Opening Valves and Metering Bag Opening Valves. Continuous Airbag Opening Valves enable a steady and uninterrupted flow of gas to inflate the airbag upon deployment, ensuring rapid inflation for optimal occupant safety. In contrast, Metering Bag Opening Valves control the inflation process by regulating gas flow, allowing for a more controlled deployment suited for specific scenarios, such as minimizing impact forces. Both types are essential for vehicle safety systems.</p></p>
