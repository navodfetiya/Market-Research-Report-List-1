<p><p>The neurovascular stents market includes specialized devices designed for use in the vascular structures of the brain. Carotid artery stents are used to treat stenosis or narrowing of the carotid arteries, helping to restore blood flow and reduce stroke risk. Intracranial stents, on the other hand, are employed to treat intracranial aneurysms or vascular malformations, providing support to weakened blood vessel walls.
