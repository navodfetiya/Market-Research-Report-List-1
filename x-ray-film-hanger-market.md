<p><ul><li>Channel Type</li><li>Clip Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The X-ray film hanger market comprises two main types: channel type and clip type. Channel type hangers feature an elongated groove where films can slide in securely, ensuring stable positioning during processing. In contrast, clip type hangers utilize clips to grip the edges of the film, providing flexibility in handling various film sizes. Both types serve the critical purpose of suspending X-ray films during drying or viewing, enhancing efficiency in medical imaging workflows and improving the quality of diagnostic results.</p></p>
