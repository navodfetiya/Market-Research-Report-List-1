<p><p>The bench-top sterilize equipment market encompasses various sterilization methods, including steam, plasma, hot air, and others. Steam sterilizers utilize high-pressure steam to eliminate microorganisms, making them ideal for medical instruments. Plasma sterilizers use low-temperature hydrogen peroxide plasma, suitable for heat-sensitive items. Hot air sterilizers employ dry heat to achieve sterilization, effective for glassware and metal tools. Other methods may include chemical and ultraviolet sterilization technologies, catering to diverse healthcare and laboratory needs with varying efficiency and application suitability.</p></p>
