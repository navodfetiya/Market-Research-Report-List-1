<p><p>The stretch wrap film market is divided into two main types: machine stretch film and hand stretch wrap. Machine stretch film is designed for use with automated wrapping machines, offering efficiency and consistency for high-volume packaging. It is typically thicker and provides superior load stability. On the other hand, hand stretch wrap is manually applied and is ideal for lower volume operations or smaller loads. It is lighter, easier to maneuver, and is popular for its flexibility and cost-effectiveness in various applications.</p></p>
