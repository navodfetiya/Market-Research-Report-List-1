<p><strong>The Fillable Flood Barrier Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gas Filling</li><li>Liquid/Solid Filling</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fillable flood barrier market comprises three main types based on the filling material: gas, liquid, and solid. Gas-filling barriers utilize air or inert gases for inflation, providing lightweight and easy deployment. Liquid-filling barriers rely on water or other fluids for stability and effective sealing against water intrusion, while solid-filling barriers use materials like sand or gravel to create a robust, permanent structure. Each type offers distinct advantages in terms of installation, weight, and effectiveness in flood protection scenarios.</p></p>
