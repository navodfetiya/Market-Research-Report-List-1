<p><p>The MRI Contrast Media Injector market features two primary types: single-head and dual-head contrast injectors. Single-head contrast injectors are designed for straightforward applications, delivering contrast media through one injector head, making them suitable for simpler diagnostic procedures. In contrast, dual-head contrast injectors offer enhanced efficiency, allowing simultaneous injection of multiple contrast agents or faster delivery rates, which is beneficial for complex imaging scenarios. Both types aim to improve diagnostic accuracy and patient comfort during MRI procedures.</p></p>
