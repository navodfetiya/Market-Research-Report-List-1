<p><ul><li>External Heated Soldering Iron</li><li>Internal Heated Soldering Iron</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric soldering iron market comprises two main types: external heated and internal heated soldering irons. External heated soldering irons feature a heating element located outside the tip, providing quicker temperature adjustments and easier maintenance. In contrast, internal heated soldering irons have the heating element embedded within the tip, offering consistent temperature control and improved heat retention for precision work. Each type is suitable for different applications, catering to various user preferences, skill levels, and project requirements in electronics and soldering tasks.</p></p>
