<p><p>A high-pressure scroll compressor is a type of positive displacement compressor that uses two interleaved scrolls to compress refrigerants or gases. These compressors are widely employed in applications requiring high efficiency and compact design, such as in HVAC systems, refrigeration units, and various industrial processes.
