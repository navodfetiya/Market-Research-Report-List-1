<p><ul><li>Single Component</li><li>Double Component</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The transparent silicone sealants market comprises two main types: single component and double component sealants. Single component sealants are ready-to-use products that cure upon exposure to moisture in the air, making them convenient for various applications such as sealing and bonding. In contrast, double component sealants consist of two parts that must be mixed before application, providing enhanced strength and durability for demanding environments. Both types serve essential roles in construction, automotive, and electronics industries, ensuring effective sealing solutions.</p></p>
