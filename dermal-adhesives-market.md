<p><strong>The Dermal Adhesives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Synthetic</li><li>Natural</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dermal adhesives market is primarily categorized into synthetic and natural types. Synthetic dermal adhesives, often made from polymers like cyanoacrylate, offer rapid bonding and are widely used in surgical applications for wound closure. They provide durability and resistance to moisture. In contrast, natural dermal adhesives, derived from biological materials such as collagen or fibrin, promote healing and are more biocompatible, minimizing potential allergic reactions. Both types cater to diverse medical needs, influencing their usage based on specific clinical scenarios.</p></p>
