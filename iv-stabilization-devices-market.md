<p><p>IV stabilization devices encompass various types designed to secure medical tubing and improve patient safety. Abdominal drainage tube securement devices maintain the position of drainage tubes post-surgery. Epidural stabilization devices secure epidural catheters for pain management. Chest drainage tube stabilization devices ensure proper placement of thoracic drainage tubes, preventing complications and enhancing patient comfort. Each device plays a crucial role in effective medical care.</p></p>
