<p><p>The enzymes in food processing market includes various types, each serving distinct functions. Proteases break down proteins, enhancing meat tenderness and improving flavor. Lactase aids lactose digestion in dairy products. Lipase contributes to flavor development in fats and oils, while xylanase helps in improving dough properties and fiber breakdown. Other enzymes cater to diverse food applications, enhancing quality and efficiency.</p></p>
