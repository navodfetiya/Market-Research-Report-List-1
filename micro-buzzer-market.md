<p><strong>The Micro Buzzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical Buzzer</li><li>Electromechanical Buzzer</li><li>Piezoelectric Buzzer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The micro buzzer market is categorized into three primary types: mechanical buzzers, electromechanical buzzers, and piezoelectric buzzers. Mechanical buzzers generate sound through a vibrating diaphragm using an electromagnetic coil. Electromechanical buzzers utilize a combination of mechanical components and electrical signals for sound production. Piezoelectric buzzers rely on the piezoelectric effect, where electrical signals induce mechanical vibrations, producing sound. These different types cater to various applications, including alarms, notifications, and consumer electronics, each offering distinct advantages in terms of size, power consumption, and sound quality.</p></p>
