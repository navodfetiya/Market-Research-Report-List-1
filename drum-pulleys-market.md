<p><p>The drum pulleys market is primarily categorized into support pulleys and drive pulleys. Support pulleys are designed to guide and support the belt in a conveyor system, ensuring proper alignment and minimizing wear. Drive pulleys, on the other hand, are responsible for transmitting power and driving the conveyor belt forward. Both types are essential for efficient material handling, with applications across various industries, including mining, manufacturing, and logistics, facilitating the smooth and effective movement of goods.</p></p>
