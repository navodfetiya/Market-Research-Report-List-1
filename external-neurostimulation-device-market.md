<p><p>The external neurostimulation device market includes various types, primarily focusing on Transcutaneous Electrical Nerve Stimulation (TENS) and Transcranial Magnetic Stimulation (TMS). TENS involves delivering low-voltage electrical currents through the skin to alleviate pain and is widely used in pain management. In contrast, TMS employs magnetic fields to stimulate nerve cells in the brain, often utilized in treating depression and other neurological disorders. Both technologies offer non-invasive treatment options, catering to a growing demand for alternative therapeutic solutions.</p></p>
