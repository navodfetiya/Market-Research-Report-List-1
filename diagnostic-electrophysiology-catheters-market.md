<p><strong>The Diagnostic Electrophysiology Catheters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Circular Mapping Catheters</li><li>Grid Mapping Catheters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The diagnostic electrophysiology catheters market includes two primary types: circular mapping catheters and grid mapping catheters. Circular mapping catheters are designed to create a detailed electrical map of the heart by collecting data from a circular array of electrodes, facilitating precise identification of arrhythmias. In contrast, grid mapping catheters feature a more extensive arrangement of electrodes that allows for comprehensive spatial mapping of cardiac electrical activity, enhancing the accuracy of diagnosis and treatment planning for various arrhythmias.</p></p>
