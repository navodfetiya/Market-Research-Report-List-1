<p><p>A Gravity Grease Interceptor (GGI) is a plumbing device designed to separate and trap grease, fats, oils, and solids from wastewater before it enters the municipal sewer system. These interceptors utilize gravity to allow lighter materials to rise and be retained in a separate chamber while heavier solids settle at the bottom. GGIs are essential for commercial kitchens and food service establishments, preventing clogs and reducing the risk of sewer backups.</p><p>The Gravity Grease Interceptor Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by the increasing demand for efficient wastewater management systems in the food and beverage industry. Regulatory pressures on businesses to comply with environmental standards are prompting the adoption of GGIs. Moreover, the rising awareness of environmental sustainability and the importance of maintaining hygiene in food preparation areas are further fueling market growth.</p><p>Technological advancements, including the integration of sensors and automated cleaning systems, are among the latest trends enhancing GGI efficiency and ease of maintenance.
