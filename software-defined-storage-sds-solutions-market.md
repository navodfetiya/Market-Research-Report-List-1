<p><p>Software Defined Storage (SDS) solutions can be categorized into two primary market types: Cloud-based and On-premises. Cloud-based SDS offers scalability and flexibility, allowing businesses to leverage storage resources over the internet without significant hardware investments. It is ideal for organizations with fluctuating storage needs. In contrast, On-premises SDS involves deploying storage solutions within the organization's infrastructure, providing greater control and security for sensitive data. This type is preferable for enterprises with stringent compliance requirements or those seeking to optimize existing hardware assets.</p></p>
