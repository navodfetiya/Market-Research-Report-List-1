<p><p>Debt management software is available in two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. This option is ideal for businesses seeking scalability and remote access. On-premise software, on the other hand, is installed locally on a company's servers, providing greater control over data security and customization. This model is suitable for organizations with specific regulatory requirements or those preferring in-house management.</p></p>
