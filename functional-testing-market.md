<p><p>Functional testing involves verifying that software applications function as intended. The market for functional testing is divided into two main types: manual and automated. Manual testing relies on human testers to execute test cases, providing flexibility and adaptability, especially in exploratory scenarios. On the other hand, automated testing utilizes specialized tools to execute scripts, enabling faster and repeated testing, making it suitable for large-scale projects. Both approaches aim to ensure software quality and functionality, catering to different testing needs and environments.</p></p>
