<p><ul><li>Air Horn</li><li>Electronic Horn</li><li>Electromagnetic Horns</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive horns and fanfares market is categorized into three main types: air horns, electronic horns, and electromagnetic horns. Air horns utilize compressed air to produce a powerful sound, often used in larger vehicles for greater audibility. Electronic horns generate sound through electrical circuits, offering a variety of tones and typically being more compact. Electromagnetic horns operate by using electromagnetic fields to vibrate a diaphragm, producing sound. Each type serves distinct applications, balancing factors like sound intensity, size, and power consumption.</p></p>
