<p><p>The freezer storage bags market comprises three primary types: polyethylene (PE), polyvinyl chloride (PVC), and polyvinylidene chloride (PVDC). PE bags are popular for their flexibility and resistance to moisture and chemicals, making them ideal for short-term storage. PVC bags offer good barrier properties and durability but may release harmful substances over time. PVDC bags provide superior barrier protection against oxygen and moisture, making them suitable for long-term freezer storage and preserving food quality for extended periods.</p></p>
