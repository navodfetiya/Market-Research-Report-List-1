<p><p>HVAC valve actuators control the flow of air and fluid in heating, ventilation, and air conditioning systems. Floating actuators provide two-position control, turning valves fully open or closed, while modulating actuators adjust flow based on varying signals. Pneumatic actuators use air pressure for operation, whereas proportional actuators offer continuous adjustments for precise control. Other types may include electric or smart actuators, which integrate with building management systems for enhanced efficiency and automation, catering to diverse application needs.</p></p>
