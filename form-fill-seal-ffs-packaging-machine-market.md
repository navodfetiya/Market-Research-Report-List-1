<p><p>Form Fill Seal (FFS) packaging machines are categorized into vertical and horizontal types based on their operation orientation. Vertical FFS machines operate in an upright position, utilizing gravity for filling and are ideal for packaging granular products, liquids, and powders. They are often used in the food and beverage sectors. In contrast, horizontal FFS machines operate on a flat plane, suitable for larger and bulkier products, and are commonly employed for items like snacks and pet food. Each type serves specific packaging needs efficiently.</p></p>
