<p><p>The Visibility Sensors market encompasses several types designed to measure atmospheric visibility. Forward Scatter Sensors utilize a laser to determine particle concentration, providing real-time visibility data. Transmissometer Sensors gauge light transmission over a defined distance, ideal for assessing visibility in fog or haze. LIDAR Sensors employ laser pulses to evaluate the intensity of atmospheric particles, offering detailed visibility analysis. Other market segments may include sensors with unique technologies or hybrid systems, catering to diverse applications across industries such as transportation and meteorology.</p></p>
