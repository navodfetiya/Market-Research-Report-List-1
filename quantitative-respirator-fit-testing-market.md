<p><p>The Quantitative Respirator Fit Testing market includes several testing methods to ensure mask efficacy in protecting against airborne contaminants. The PortaCount test utilizes laser-based technology to measure the particle concentration inside and outside the respirator, providing accurate fit assessments. The CNC (Controlled Negative Pressure) test evaluates facepiece leakage through a series of pressure changes, while the CNP (Controlled Negative Pressure) test measures the respirator's seal by creating a vacuum around the mask. Each method helps ensure optimal respiratory protection in various industries.</p></p>
