<p><p>The coal pulverizers market is segmented into three main types based on speed: Low Speed, Middle Speed, and High Speed coal pulverizers. Low Speed pulverizers operate at less than 20 RPM and are typically used in large-scale power plants, offering high reliability. Middle Speed pulverizers function between 20-100 RPM, providing a balance of efficiency and control. High Speed pulverizers, operating above 100 RPM, are characterized by their compact design and quick response, suitable for smaller applications and specialized processing.</p></p>
