<p><p>The automotive refinish paint market consists of several key types: </p><p>1. **Primer and Base Coat**: Primers prepare surfaces for paint adhesion, providing a smooth foundation, while base coats deliver the desired color and finish.</p><p>2. **Top Coat**: This layer enhances durability, offering protection against environmental factors and UV rays, and can provide additional color richness.</p><p>3. **Clear Coat**: The final layer, clear coat protects the underlying paint from scratches and fading, ensuring longevity and a glossy appearance. Each type plays a critical role in achieving a quality automotive finish.</p></p>
