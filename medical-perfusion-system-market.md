<p><ul><li>Cardiopulmonary Perfusion</li><li>Cell Perfusion</li><li>Ex Vivo Organ Perfusion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical perfusion system market comprises several key types. Cardiopulmonary perfusion involves the support of patients' heart and lung functions during surgeries, typically using bypass machines. Cell perfusion focuses on delivering nutrients and oxygen to cells in research and therapeutic contexts, enhancing cell behavior and viability. Ex vivo organ perfusion preserves and nourishes organs outside the body for transplantation, improving the viability of donor organs. Each type plays a crucial role in advancing medical treatments and research.</p></p>
