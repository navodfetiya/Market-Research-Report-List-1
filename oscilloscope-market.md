<p><p>The oscilloscope market is primarily divided into two main types: handheld oscilloscopes and desktop oscilloscopes. Handheld oscilloscopes are portable devices designed for field use, offering convenience and flexibility for technicians on-the-go. They typically have limited bandwidth and features compared to their desktop counterparts. Desktop oscilloscopes, on the other hand, are more powerful and feature-rich, suitable for laboratory and industrial applications. They provide high bandwidth, advanced analysis capabilities, and larger screens, making them ideal for detailed waveform analysis and diagnostics.</p></p>
