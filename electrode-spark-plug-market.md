<p><ul><li>Hot Sparkplug</li><li>Cold Sparkplug</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electrode spark plug market can be categorized into hot and cold spark plugs, which are differentiated by their heat range and intended applications. Hot spark plugs retain heat effectively, which aids in preventing fouling in low-compression engines, making them ideal for older vehicles and specific applications. Cold spark plugs, conversely, dissipate heat more quickly, making them suitable for high-performance engines that operate at elevated temperatures. Each type serves distinct engine requirements, influencing performance and efficiency.</p></p>
