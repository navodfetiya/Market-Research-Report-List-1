<p><p>The industrial refrigeration heat exchanger market is categorized into movable and stationary heat exchangers. In contrast, stationary heat exchangers are fixed in place, offering consistency and reliability for long-term operations. Both types facilitate efficient thermal energy transfer in refrigeration systems, enhancing energy efficiency and overall system performance in industries like food processing, pharmaceuticals, and chemical manufacturing.</p></p>
