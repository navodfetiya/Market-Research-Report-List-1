<p><ul><li>Electromagnetic Induction Way</li><li>Magnetic Resonance(NMR) Method</li><li>Electromagnetic Coupling Way</li><li>Microwave Resonant Mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cell phone wireless charger market comprises several technologies. Electromagnetic induction uses coils to transfer energy through magnetic fields, suitable for short distances. Magnetic resonance (NMR) allows for higher efficiency over longer distances with resonant coupling. Electromagnetic coupling is a broader category that includes various methods of energy transfer utilizing magnetic fields. Microwave resonant mode employs microwave radiation for charging over greater distances but requires specialized equipment. Each method offers distinct advantages in efficiency, range, and compatibility with devices.</p></p>
