Leaf chains are typically used in lifting mechanisms due to their strength and flexibility. Roller chains are commonly found in bicycles and industrial machinery for efficient power transfer. Conveyor chains facilitate the movement of goods in manufacturing and logistics. Together, these chains serve various industries efficiently.</p></p>
